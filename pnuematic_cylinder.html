Pnuematic cylinder

STANDARD PNEUMATIC CYLINDERS.

  • Pneumatic cylinders use the force of air to move in both extends and retract strokes. They have two ports to allow air in, one for outstroke and one for instroke.
  • Stroke length for this design is not limited. We offer standard cylinders from 32mm bore to 150 mm bore with stroke range from 25mm to & as per customer requirements.

CUSTOMISED CYLINDERS.

  • Customized Pneumatic Cylinder which is customized as per the specifications of our clients.
  • SBE offers various customised cylinders from 32mm bore to 350 mm bore with stroke range up to 2200 mm.
