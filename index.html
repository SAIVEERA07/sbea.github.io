Pnueamatic cylinders

Pneumatic cylinders use the force of air to move in both extends and retract strokes. They have two ports to allow air in, one for outstroke and one for instroke. Stroke length for this design is not limited. We offer standard cylinders from 32mm bore to 150 mm bore with stroke range from 25mm to & as per customer requirements.

Valves

Pneumatic valves are one of the components responsible for regulating the pressure, rate, and amount of air that circulates throughout a pneumatic system. We at SBE manufactures all type of pneumatic valves such as Solenoid valves, Quick Exhaust valves, Foot operated valves, Compact valves, Hand lever valves, Spool valves & etc.,
