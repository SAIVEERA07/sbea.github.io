Valves

  • Pneumatic valves are one of the components responsible for regulating the pressure, rate, and amount of air that circulates throughout a pneumatic system.
  • We at SBE manufactures all type of pneumatic valves such as Solenoid valves, Quick Exhaust valves, Foot operated valves, Compact valves, Hand lever valves, Spool valves & etc., Valves are constructed from zinc or aluminium and machined bodies.
  • Porting can be with either NPT, BSP & Metric threads to meet connection preferences. Mechanically operated valves can be either spool or poppet designs depending on valve selection. We also design special purpose valves as per customer requirements.
